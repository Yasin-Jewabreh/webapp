# 03: User Verification Strategy

## Meta

Status
: Decided

Updated
: 18-Jun-2026

## Problem Statement

The platform connects people in need of assistance with volunteer helpers. Since users may meet each other in person and exchange personal information, the project team had to decide how users should be verified.
Without any verification process, fake profiles and misuse could reduce trust in the platform. However, requiring too many verification steps could discourage users from registering.
The challenge was to find a balance between security, trust and usability.

## Decision

The team decided that users must verify both their email address and phone number during the registration process.
Additionally, all users are required to upload a profile picture when creating an account.
The email address and phone number help ensure that users can be contacted if necessary and reduce the risk of fake accounts. Requiring a profile picture increases transparency and helps build trust between helpers and people in need of assistance.
This approach was chosen because the platform is designed for real-world interactions between strangers. Therefore, establishing trust and accountability is considered an important requirement. 
The Decision was taken by: The project team

## Regarded Options

### Option 1: No Verification

# Pros
- Fast registration process
- Easy implementation
- Low barrier to entry

# Cons
- High risk of fake accounts
- Lower trust between users
- Increased risk of misuse

### Option 2: Email Verification Only

# Pros
- Easy to implement
- Basic proof of ownership

# Cons
- Limited user verification
- Phone numbers remain unverified
- Lower level of trust

### Option 3: Email Verification, Phone Verification and Required Profile Picture (Chosen Option)

# Pros
- Increased trust between users
- Better protection against fake accounts
- Improved accountability
- Greater transparency
- Practical for an MVP

# Cons
- Longer registration process
- Additional implementation effort

### Option 4: Email Verification, Phone Verification, Profile Picture and Criminal Record Verification

# Pros
- Highest level of trust
- Strong protection for vulnerable users

# Cons
- Complex registration process
- Difficult to implement
- May discourage users from registering
- Not realistic for an MVP

# Chosen Option
The team selected email verification, phone verification and a required profile picture because it provides a good balance between user safety, trust and implementation effort. More advanced verification methods may be considered in future versions of the platform.

# Assessment
The regarded options were discussed within the project team during the requirements analysis phase.

The team compared the options based on the following criteria:
- User safety
- Trust between users
- Risk of misuse
- Registration effort
- Technical feasibility
